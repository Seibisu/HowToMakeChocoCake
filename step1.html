Step 1: Get Ingredients

Ingredients for Cake:

  • 8 eggs
  • 200 g flour
  • 2 tsps baking powder
  • 30 g cocoa
  • 160 g sugar
  • 2 sachets vanilla sugar
  • 100 ml milk

Chocolate Cream:

  • 500 g whipping cream
  • 330 g baking chocolate
  • 60 g chopped chocolate

Decorations:

  • Chocolate or chocolate sprinkles
  • Some whipped cream
